Consent Narrative

Who We Are

We are students from Olin College of Engineering taking a class in User Experience Design. Our team is working on a semester long project about indoor navigation, and exploring user interaction with software. Weâ€™d like to speak to you about your experiences navigating and directing people in your space. We also might and collect information using the techniques cited below.

What We Are Doing

We would love a chance to have a 30 to 45 minute conversation with you. If you are willing to meet with us again, we may request follow-up sessions for different stages of the project, such as prototype feedback or cooperative design activities.

There are a number of ways we might keep records of our conversation, which follow. We will ask before collecting any information, and you are free to refuse any that you do not feel comfortable with.

Information collection techniques:

  • Taking notes
  • Taking photos
  • Recording audio
  • Recording video
  • Artifacts from cooperative design

Confidentiality

  • We will be anonymizing all identifying information; pseudonyms will be used to refer to any participants
  • We will not share any information participants ask us not to share
  • We will ask before taking or using photos of participants
  • Contact information will be kept separate from interview information
  • Information in our team work space is kept secure

Benefits

You will get the chance to see and participate in the engineering and design process and see your feedback incorporated into the project. Unfortunately, we cannot compensate you for your time, but we hope that you will gain satisfaction from the process.

Risks

At worst, your expectations will not be met. Your contact information, including name, will not be shared.
